Common Questions

Questions about this service

Do we need to clean up our data before you can start?+
Not at all. We assess what you have during discovery and design the project around your actual data situation. Improving data quality is often something we tackle as part of the engagement.
What tools do you use to build dashboards?+
We use the right tool for your context, which may include Power BI, Looker Studio, or custom web dashboards depending on your team size, existing software, and preferences.
How long does a typical analytics project take?+
A focused first dashboard covering your key metrics can be ready within three to four weeks. Larger analytics programs with multiple departments are scoped individually.
Can we add new data sources later?+
Yes. Everything we build is designed to grow. Adding new sources, new metrics, or new dashboards later is straightforward because we build on solid foundations from the start.
