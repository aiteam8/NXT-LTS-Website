Common Questions

Questions about this service

What if our data lives in multiple systems?+
We create a data layer that connects all sources, standardizes definitions, and feeds a single dashboard. Complexity is on our side, not yours.
Can each person see customized views?+
Yes. Role-based access ensures each person or team sees only what is relevant for their role.
How often is the dashboard updated?+
Depending on your needs, dashboards can update in real-time, hourly, or daily. We agree on frequency during scoping.
Can we request changes post-launch?+
Yes. Dashboards evolve as your business changes, with post-launch support and optional ongoing development.
